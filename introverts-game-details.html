Introvert vs. World

Project information

I have an idea for a game named Introvert vs. World (working title), in which the player controls an introvert (commonly referred to as an I) trying to navigate a world full of extroverts (Es), which act as the antagonist of the story. There is more to the plot, but I thought I would get started by trying my hand at pixel art, using Piskel. Below are nine items that a stereotypical I would own, including some that can be found in my own room.
