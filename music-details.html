Musical compositions

Project information

The following are some of my musical compositions, all made in Cakewalk by BandLab.

I have selected these three as I believe they showcase the following abilities of mine:

  • creating original compositions for video games
  • creating original compositions with regular instruments
  • creatively making use of VSTs to remix existing songs

"Cheaptune"

Alexandra Wonyu · Cheaptune

VST used: NES VST by Matt Montag

In the RPG I will one day make, this is what I imagine to be background music to some runaway sequence in a forest, the likes of what the player experiences early on in Mother 3.

"Veronica"

Alexandra Wonyu · Veronica

VSTs used: Mini Grand by AIR, Ample Bass P Lite by Ample Sound, MT Power Drum Kit by MANDA AUDIO

This is the instrumental version of one of the first songs I wrote to completion, originally on the guitar, and further brought to life by the power of MIDI and a smooth bass line.

"Panorama (8-bit version)"

Alexandra Wonyu · Panorama (8-Bit)

VST used: NES VST by Matt Montag

This is a chiptune remix of one of my favourite songs in C major: "파노라마 (Panorama)" by Lee Chan-hyuk of AKMU, complete with my favourite riff from 2022.
